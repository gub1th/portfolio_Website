About me

I'm Daniel and I'm a

Hi! That's my finger, but not my dog. Anyways, I am an undergraduate sophomore studying Information Systems at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm passionate about anything that intersects the areas of business and technology. I feel that my biggest strength is my diverse skillset and well-roundedness; I've learned more about the development and improvement of products as a Product Team Intern at Media Nusantara Citra, where I closely shadowed a product manager who was in charge of the finance and banking app 'Motionpay'. I've gained programming and software development knowledge through my time as a Research Assistant for a High Frequency Proprietary Stock Trader, where I developed data-extraction and data-manipulation scripts along with a designated GUI. I've developed data analysis and communication skills in the areas of business as a Business Development Intern for Maju Makmur Sempurna, where I analyzed operational data involved in the logistical process and conducted internal research for a to-be integrated information system. So despite not being sure of what I will do in the future (technically, noone is!), I am well prepared to face whatever challenge that is bound to appear before me.

Aside from that, I enjoy playing sports for fun. I play almost everything, but my favorites as of now are basketball, volleyball and tennis. I've been playing tennis for about 12 years now, and have competed both on national and international levels for juniors. I have a blog which documents my thoughts and perspectives throughout my tennis (and life!) journey, which you can access by clicking here. I also make content for fun sometimes, which you can see on my Youtube channel and Tiktok page.
