Overview

Winter 2019/2020

Vacancy is one of the first medium/long-term projects that I've worked on. This was done as my Computer Science Internal Assessment, where I had to identify a real-world client and develop a real-world solution that was applicable to his needs. The project had multiple stages, including Planning, Solution Overview, Development, Evaluation and Video.

Essentially, it is a room booking system that is customized for the music department at Beacon Academy, which has allowed for the reservation process to become much more convenient.
