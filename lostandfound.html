Overview

Fall 2021

Lost and Found is a project I worked on as a member of CMU's Scottylabs organization. I wanted to get involved with something that had more of a practical application, and this was a good avenue in doing so. For this project, my role was a programmer, where I was mostly involved with basic front-end development. This app is pretty self-explanatory, given the name. It is a website that helps Carnegie Mellon students find or bring awareness to lost items on campus. This alleviates the issue that physical Lost and Founds have, where these centers are not in-sync with each other in terms of tracking/logging items. Centralizing all the data in a single website fixes this issue, where students do not need to visit each center individually, and can more conveniently find/report an item.
