Overview

Fall 2021

Empyrean was a project that I helped develop during my first semester as a member of CMU's Game Creation Society, where I took on the role of a gameplay programmer. As a team of around 15 people with varying roles, we met every Sunday for 2-4 hours to work on our tasks.

The game is a 3D first-person shooter multiplayer and follows a battle royale-type genre, where the aim is to survive while killing all the other enemies. Empyrean has a steampunk-centric theme, embodied through the map, character, and weapon design.
