Overview

Spring 2020

I made this project during Hack112, which was a single day Hackathon. My team was the 'Grey Giants', which consisted of myself along with 3 other people. What was interesting this time was the fact that my team selection was randomized, meaning that I didn't know any of the members prior to this. Being in different timezones, coordination and communication was one of the biggest challenges.

This is a simpler version of the classic and renowned 'Monopoly' game, but with a Carnegie-Mellon based theme. It has the functionalities of a normal Monopoly game, including paying rent, buying property, buying houses/hotels, and more.
